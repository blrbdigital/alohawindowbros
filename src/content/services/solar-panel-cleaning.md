## What's Included

Safe, thorough cleaning that restores your panels to peak performance:

- **Purified water system** — no chemicals, no residue, no streaks
- **Bird dropping & grime removal** — stubborn buildup safely cleared
- **Performance check** — we make sure panels are spotless and unobstructed

Dirty panels can lose **15–25% efficiency**. Regular cleaning pays for itself.

## Our Process

1. **Inspect** — We check your panels for buildup, debris, and any damage
2. **Clean** — Purified water + soft tools safely remove everything without scratching
3. **Verify** — Final check to make sure every panel is clear and producing at full capacity
