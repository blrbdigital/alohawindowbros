## What's Included

Every window washing includes a complete four-in-one clean:

- **Interior & exterior glass** — streak-free, crystal clear
- **Screens** — removed, cleaned, and reinstalled
- **Frames & sills** — wiped down and detailed
- **Skylights & hard-to-reach windows** — water-fed pole system

We handle residential and commercial properties of all sizes.

## Our Process

1. **Inspect** — We assess your windows, note any problem areas, and protect your landscaping
2. **Clean** — Eco-friendly solutions + professional tools for a streak-free finish
3. **Walk-through** — We check every window with you before we leave

## Pricing

Every home is different. Most residential jobs run **$150–$400** depending on number of windows and accessibility. We provide free on-site quotes — no surprises.
